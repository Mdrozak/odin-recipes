Przepis na leczo

Description

Leczo is a simple dish from Hungarian cuisine made of peppers, onions and tomatoes, fried in lard and seasoned with paprika powder.

This dish has become very popular in Poland and is most often cooked with sausage. In this form, it constitutes a complete meal, e.g. with bread.

Ingredients

  • 2 spoons of lard
  • 2 onions
  • 200g of sausage
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 3 peppers, yellow, red, green
  • 2 teaspoons of sweet red powdered paprica
  • salt and pepper as desired
  • 1/2 teaspoon of hot red powdered paprica
  • 1kg of tomatoes, 2 spoons of tomato concentrate

Steps

  1. Put the lard into a wide pot, add the diced onion and sliced ​​sausage, fry, stirring occasionally, for about 7 minutes. Add grated or crushed garlic and fry for another 3 minutes.
  2. Add diced peppers and fry, stirring occasionally, for about 3 minutes. Season with salt (approx. half a teaspoon), freshly ground pepper and sweet and hot paprika powder.
  3. Then add peeled and diced fresh tomatoes (without seeds from the inside of the chambers) or tomato passata. Cook for about 15 minutes with the lid ajar, stirring several times in the meantime. Finally, add tomato paste if you used fresh tomatoes.
