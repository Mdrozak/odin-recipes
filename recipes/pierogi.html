Przepis na pierogi z ziemniakami

Description

Pierogis with potatoes are traditional holiday dish within our family. Every out family member loves pierogi.

This great dish is unfortunately very time consuming to make. Therefore it should be made in great numbers

Ingredients

    Dough ingredients

    • 2 cups of wheat flour
    • 1 cup of warm water
    • 1 teaspoon of salt

    Stuffing

    • 4 big potatoes
    • 1 onion
    • 3 spoons of butter
    • salt and pepper as desired

    Additional ingredients

    • 1 onion
    • 3 spoons of butter
    • wheat flour for sprinkling the pastry board

Steps

  1. First, peel the onion, cut it and fry it in butter. Then the potatoes should be peeled, washed, boiled in salted water and drained. Then they should be crushed thoroughly. Then add the onion and butter to the potatoes and season with salt and pepper. Mix all ingredients thoroughly. Products for dumplings stuffed with potatoes should be set aside to cool.
  2. From the given ingredients for the dough for dumplings with potatoes, you need to gradually pour water and knead a soft dough.
  3. Then, on the pastry board, roll out the dough for dumplings with potatoes and onion, and cut out discs using a glass. Then you need to put the potato stuffing into the dumplings and stick the dumplings together.
  4. Then the potato dumplings should be cooked in salted water for about 5 minutes after they float. Then they need to be strained.
  5. Before serving, peel and cut the onion and fry it in butter. Finally, dumplings stuffed with potatoes should be served topped with fried onion and butter.
